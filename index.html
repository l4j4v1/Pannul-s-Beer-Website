Pannul's Beer

Discover the essence of nature in every drop

By Javiera Zuniga

Pannul's is a craft beer that has its unique aroma and taste, incorporates a range of natural and distinctive elements, offering an adventurous and memorable experience to beer enthusiasts. Our brand is eco-friendly, reliable, close to the customers and sustentable. This beer is based on Cerro Panul, located in La Florida, southeastern part of Santiago, here you can find a large number of restaurants, shopping centers, parks, bike paths, and schools.

Background

La Florida hosts a dense population due to its central location and the variety of services it offers to the community. Furthermore, it has strong connectivity with other areas of Santiago. Unfortunately, La Florida lacks university institutions within its territory, leading to a limited student population in this area. With Pannul's Beer, we aim to attract this university audience through our app, which offers incredible discounts to students, as well as promoting sports and charity. It is expected that the presence of students could have a positive impact on the local economy, rejuvenate its streets, and promote integration between students and the local residents.

Promotional Video

This video aims to attract students who feel stressed from their everyday university life and want to try something that will break their routine for a moment, something that will take them on a journey and make them feel free.

How it works!

This app operates through points you accumulate as you walk. With these points, you can redeem discount coupons for your next purchase of Pannul's beer or donate your points to charitable campaigns. Additionally, students receive additional discounts.

The Campaign

Many university students go out for drinks after classes, which is why Pannul's is a refreshing and affordable choice for beer and nature enthusiasts. Also, 50% of the profits will be donated to reforest Panul forest, which will attract more interest from those who want to contribute to environmental improvement.

Why support us

A beer rooted in the community of La Florida with a strong commitment to reforesting Cerro Paul, presents a unique and valuable opportunity. This product captures the essence and identity of La Florida, establishing a strong sense of belonging and connection with the local community. This product promote environmental sustainability, attract a specific target audience, revitalize the local community and create a lasting impact on multiple levels. It is a business opportunity with a purpose beyond commerce, an investment in the community and the environment that yields long-term benefits for all involved.
